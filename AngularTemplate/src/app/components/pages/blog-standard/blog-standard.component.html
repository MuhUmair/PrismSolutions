Project Development

Before we begin, we take time to pinpoint what issue the project is actually supposed to fix. It’s not always obvious. We identify the deliverables and project planning steps required to meet the project's goals, e.g. What are the specific outputs we're expected to produce? Next, we estimate due dates for each deliverable in our project . We finalize these dates when we sit down with the client to define our project schedule.


Then we look at each deliverable and define the series of tasks that must be completed to accomplish each one. For each task, we determine the amount of time it will take, the resources necessary, and who will be responsible for execution.

Project Management

Prism Solutions assign a Project Manager that is responsible for managing the progress of your project. The project manager will be one of your primary contacts and they will provide you with status updates, schedule meetings, communicate deadlines, and coordinate internal team meetings. Prism Solutions uses a combination of cloud based technologies and internal procedures to ensure that you and your team are kept up to date with project progress in real time.

Additionally, throughout the project we will hold weekly recurring meetings to provide you with updates on recent items completed and pending items to be completed. These meetings will include the project lead from your organization, your Prism Solutions Project Manager, and depending on the scope of your project additional Prism Solutions team members.

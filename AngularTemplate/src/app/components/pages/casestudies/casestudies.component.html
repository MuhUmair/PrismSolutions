Case Study

Bogie Investment Group, Inc.

Sales Cloud, Service Cloud

How a mid-sized Commercial Real Estate company earned 35% more leads and boosted sales by 27%

After nearly two decades in business, sales and leads generation at Bogie Investment Group, Inc. were stagnating. Despite a strong reputation and well-respected suite of real estate services, the company was still relying on the same outdated processes for capturing, nurturing, and converting leads to sales. The team knew it had to modernize its customer relationship management strategy to keep up with a highly competitive market full of tech-oriented disruptors. It was finally time to modernize the sales and lead generation process.

About Bogie Investment Group, Inc: The Bogie Group of Marcus & Millichap is a full-service team of commercial real estate agents and support staff specializing in the brokerage of multifamily real estate investments and 1031 exchanges in the Long Beach, South Bay, Los Angeles and surrounding areas. The Bogie Group provides access to the industry's largest inventory of exclusively listed properties, cutting-edge research, a nationwide network of more than 2,000 agents connected by proprietary technology, and more.

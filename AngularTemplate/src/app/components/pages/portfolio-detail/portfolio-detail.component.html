Project Overview

Salesforce Customer Communities and Sales Cloud Implementation
The Bogie Group is a full-service commercial Real Estate company specializing in the brokerage of multifamily real estate investments and 1031 exchanges. The System was migrated from manual excel sheets to Salesforce Cloud Communities. The new system is a combination of CRM & property search tools and is solely build for Bogie Investment groups, Inc. The focus of this project was to develop a commercial Real Estate Software System in Salesforce that would reflect actual business processes of the company.

Project Impact

The Prism Consulting team implemented Salesforce Sales Cloud integration to provide Sales Agents everything; from lead generation tools to cold calling, communication to proposal management, and analyzing different types of data & reports, generation tools & other open-house management tools. All Sales agents are at one forum now for effective collaboration. Processes have been speed up and quality of cold calling is improved due to process automation which makes deal making easy and fast.

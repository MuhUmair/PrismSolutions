How a Commercial Cleaning and Insulation Company eliminated 80% of their manual efforts and managed operations by 50% more efficiently

A cleaning and insulation company requires a scalable approach to manage customers, processes, products, data and customers’ orders. They require a highly automated and user-friendly Process and Order Management System which auto-links data with customers.

Client Business Background

The client is a Cleaning and Insulation Company in the Bay Area since 2000 with the goal of creating a safer, healthier, and more beautiful world. Their ecosystem consists of one-stop-solution for attic & crawl space cleaning & insulation needs.

Client’s Current Operating Environment & Change They Required

The client manages their products listing and order management through manual process. It caused delays, manual errors, and mismanagement.


Client required developing a full-cycled Process Management tool with Salesforce environment. In order to keep track of orders at multiple locations, the client needs a well-organized order management process. They need a cloud based system with features like: Order Management, Lead Management, Resource Allocation Tool, Product/Service Management and Payment Integration.

Solution and Approach

Our Team after studying the objectives built a solution for the client’s requirement of Order and Process Management System top of Salesforce Sales Cloud. The solution consists of Salesforce provided standard functionalities along with customized configurations as per requirements.


The solution allows to auto manage multiple orders simultaneously. It saves lots of time of approaching customers manually and keeping record of their orders. Using customized configurations in Salesforce environment process management meets the requirement of products, orders, reorders updates along with alarm notifications to the system user. Implementing a custom billing system allows a single page data entry and links data to customers, order sheets, and multiple other areas in the backend.


With Salesforce provided standard & custom Reports and Dashboard, the client is accessible to real-time data, analytics, statistics overall operations of the ecosystem. Overall delivered solution is scalable, consistent, automated, customized, and highly integrated with Salesforce solution to manage entire business metrics.

Key Benefits

Beyond the immediate efficiency gains and boosted sales, this new Salesforce implementation unlocked groundbreaking insights into sales team performance. The team at The Bogie Group can now identify gaps, trends, and future projections based on q ualitative customer data. The company has stabilized its place in the market and has positioned itself well to continue scaling into the future.

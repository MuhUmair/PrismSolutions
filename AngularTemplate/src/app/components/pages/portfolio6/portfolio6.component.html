Challenge

Most shippers rely on manual check calls and other complex methods to provide tracking details to their customers. Many transportation management systems (TMS) that offer track and trace functionality still depend on manual processes. For time efficient processes, our client requested that we implement a Freight Tracking System. The main goal was to automate the process of order tracking.

Challenge

Client is a delivery logistics company that allows to deliver products with accuracy and consistency. For order tracking, most shippers rely on manual check calls and other complex methods to provide tracking details to their customers. Many transportation management systems (TMS) that offer track and trace functionality still depend on manual processes. For time efficient processes, our client requested that we implement a Freight Tracking System with a focus to automate the process of order tracking for best customer experience.


Project Overview

Salesforce Sales Cloud Implementation
Client wanted Prism Solutions to build an app to allocate a tracking number to every order so that customers are able to track their parcels thorugh that number. Salesforce consultant team at Prism Solutions developed Order Tracking tool in Salesforce Sales Cloud environment. Project Impact

The delivered 'Freight Tracking System' captures the geographic location and coordinates with each delivery location, verifies it instantly, and records it for accuracy. Sales team can instantly create unique shipment tracking numbers for customers. No more waiting and guessing on order ETAs or other shipping related inquiries. With the help of Order Ttracking System Client's customers can track their purchasing and procurement activities, such as:

  1. Confirm purchased orders are received
  2. Know where the orders are
  3. Keep track of multiple delivery dates
  4. The reassurance you'll meet the order deadline
  5. Simplify purchase order reporting

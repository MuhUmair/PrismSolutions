Dedicated Resource Centre

At Prism Solutions, hire a dedicated resource to establish your own offshore development center, expand your development and testing infrastructure and build a team of your offshore call center agents. Our appointed resources will work on your projects to fulfill your needs and help you in reducing operating costs and improving the productivity of your organization.


We provide the best infrastructure and administration for your resources in our Resource Centre. Our offshore development teams develop the software as per your requirements and goals, so, you don’t need to worry about anything other than building your business. We believe in providing a cost-effective and long-term strategic partnership with our clients from various industries.

Why Prism Solutions as Dedicated Resource Centre?

Instead of having a whole IT department that will disturb your financial budget, partnering with our offshore team will allow you to complete your projects, improve and maintain your resources professionally, quickly and effectively. Our dedicated resource center is exclusively designed to help organizations with the perfect resources and infrastructure to deploy large IT and non-IT projects.

Our Dedicated Resource Model

What makes our dedicated business resource model unbeatable is its flexibility. As we know, the requirements of clients constantly change, and we adjust to these changes any time before delivery. At The One Technologies, our Dedicated Resource Models propose competitive prices, dedicated resources, no hidden costs, and zero overheads to every client.


  • Full-Time Hiring - Hire expert resources for your projects on a full-time basis and have full control over the process.
  • Part-Time Hiring - As the name implies in part-time hiring model, to cater to small business needs, you can hire a small team of dedicated developers for small projects.
  • Hourly Hiring - Hire our expert based on the number of working hours. For small projects and to cater to small business needs, this model works on an hourly basis.
  • Fixed Price - This model is well suited to clients who have a well-structured process to gather requirements, scope, deliverables and acceptance criteria for the project.
  • Dedicated Team - We provide experienced and highly skilled resources to work on a monthly hiring basis for your project development work.

Case Study

Freight Print

Sales Cloud

How a tailored Salesforce solution helped a Logistics Company improve operational efficiency by 40%

The delivery logistics company required technology to build a Freight Tracking System in Salesforce environment. They required this platform to allocate a tracking number to every order so that customers are able to track their parcels.

Client Business Background

The Client is a delivery logistics company that allows delivering products with accuracy and consistency. Company’s aim is to improve shipper's efficiency in handling and managing their deliveries to their customers.

Client’s Current Operating Environment & Change They Required

For order tracking, most shippers rely on manual check calls and other complex methods to provide tracking details to their customers. Many transportation management systems (TMS) that offer track and trace functionality still depend on manual processes. For time efficient processes, client requested that we implement a Freight Tracking System with a focus to automate the process of order tracking for best customer experience. The client desires transparency, consistency, and automation with the system.

Solution and Approach

After studying the objectives, our Salesforce consultant team built a solution for the client’s requirements of Freight Management System in Salesforce Sales Cloud environment. The implemented solution allows customers to confirm order is placed, know where the orders are, keep track of multiple delivery dates, and track order deadline. This is possible using Salesforce standard and custom objects.


Freight Print Order Tracking Tool is built to enhance customers’ shipping experience. Having customer relationship management (CRM) software is a key factor in improving customer relationship. Salesforce integration with Freight Management System provides a powerful tool for any logistics company that uses Salesforce. Overall, with the solution Client conducts efficient, real-time, consistent, and automated freight management processes.

Key Benefits

The client is delivered with customized, consistent, highly automated, and real-time software solution. Freight Print Order Tracking tool captures the geographic location, coordinates with each delivery location, verifies it instantly, and records it for accuracy. Sales team can instantly create unique shipment tracking numbers for customers. No more waiting and guessing on order ETAs or other shipping related inquiries. With the solution, the client ensures accurate & resilient functioning of order tracking & freight management.
